# DESIGN.md - Rank 1 CPA Visual Reference Library

Read this before designing anything. Study these sites. Absorb their patterns.
Do not copy any single site. Pull the best elements from each and synthesize something original.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS FILE

These are reference sites curated for their visual excellence.
For each site, study: typography scale, spacing, motion, hero treatment, color usage, navigation style.
Then make your own decisions. Combine what works. Discard what does not fit a premium CPA brand.

## WHAT TO EXTRACT FROM THESE SITES

Typography patterns to steal:
- Oversized display headlines with tight line height
- High contrast between display weight and body weight
- Wide letter spacing on small caps labels
- Minimal font families - one display, one body, nothing else

Motion patterns to steal:
- Fade up on scroll reveal - subtle, not dramatic
- Smooth video transitions behind hero
- Hover states that feel responsive but not jumpy
- Page load sequence that builds from top

Layout patterns to steal:
- Full bleed hero sections with centered or left aligned content
- Generous padding between sections - at least 120px
- Single column content blocks with maximum width around 800px for body
- Navigation that disappears on scroll and reappears on scroll up

Things NOT to steal from any of these sites:
- Anything colorful or playful
- Card grids
- Illustration or icon heavy sections
- Anything that looks like a startup or app

## THE GOAL

When someone lands on rank1cpa.com they should feel like they just walked into the lobby of a Goldman Sachs private wealth office. Not a co-working space. Not a startup. Not a tax prep chain.

Every design decision should ask: does this feel like it belongs in that lobby?
If yes, keep it. If no, cut it.
